Since 2008 Associate Professor at the Art Academy of Latvia, Department of ceramics.

Since 1995 Member of the Artists′ Union of Latvia.

1998 Master of Arts at the Art Academy of Latvia, Department of ceramics.

Personal exhibitions since 1992. Participating in group exhibitions since 1980.

Exhibitions in Latvia, Spain, Switzerland, Taiwan, Egypt, Russia etc.

Līga Skariņa creates artworks that impart lightness, mood and feeling to her ceramic stories in which the ancient material shows contemporary interpretations. She uses white stoneware and porcelain as a basic material. The light colour and the nuanced surfaces result from the particular ceramic technology used.

All works is made with love and passion. You can offer Your name or logo on it.
